How technology has affected us?


"The book is dead, long live the book."

Technology has its control over 21st century from health to communication, to security to ecommerce. It has introduced a lot of new, modern and useful factors by which costumer enjoys shopping. Since the last 20 years, the ecommerce industry is growing significantly. As technology transforms business-to-consumer transactions, consumers can use a variety of tools to estimate pricing, compare options, locate businesses, and receive coupons. The use of ecommerce technologies helps to increase sales performance in the virtual store through automaiton, and other things.

Digital technology have a significant impact on the traditional publishing and retailing businesses and it has also given the book a new lease on life. "Literature found itself at war with the internet," says jim hinks, digital editor of Comma press. But its exactly opposite to its expectation because both the publishers and retailers as gain a new way to bring out their books in the market. This not only is fruitful to the retailes and publishers but also to the readers.

Due to the technology, the transactions are much more simple and easier then they were before. For the customer, the credit cards and the rise of ecommerce have made it easier than ever for firms to sell their items when they want. Technology has made marketing more easier, more successful and cost-effective which helps to boost the coporate strategy. Previously, the companies were limited to advertise their goods. Whoever has a little bit of fund they used newspaper and television and radio to advertise. But other copanies couldn't do the same thing. But nowadays, internet has made our advertise pop up everywhere so that people could reach out to them. It's easier to find your target audience, understand their wants, and engage with them on a personal level when you market your business online.

Not everyone can afford to go to college. Many people who would like to further their education are unable to do so because they are employed, caring for families, or live too far away from their local institution to commute. All of that has altered thanks to online programs. Students can now enroll in online programs or take single classes at their leisure, without having to leave their homes.
